With the growing market of home computers in the late 1970s and the early 1980s, video games had become the most popular source of entertainment in the world. Currently, the prediction of the global revenue of video games in 2021 could be over $160 billion dollars. Every other year, more advanced consoles have spawned a large number of electronic games and game types. Thousands of games are available to choose from and new developers have emerged one after another, trying to seize the market share

The datasets is from Kaggle, the sources link is Video Games Sales 2019 | Kaggle, which contains 55792 rows by 23 columns. However, only 10 columns are used: name, platform, year, genre, publisher, NA_Sales, EU_Sales, JP_Sales, Other_Sales and Global_Sales.

On this page, data will be presented in different forms such as a bar chart, a multi-line chart, and a sunburst chart.

The bar chart below shows which publisher is the most popular. Once you click the two buttons to indicate the different values.
